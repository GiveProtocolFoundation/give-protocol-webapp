# Creating an Account

This guide walks you through registering a new Give Protocol account.

## Step 1 — Navigate to Registration

Go to [giveprotocol.io/register](https://giveprotocol.io/register) or click **Sign Up** from any page on the site.

## Step 2 — Choose Your Role

Select the role that best describes how you plan to use Give Protocol:

| Role          | Description                                                                      |
| ------------- | -------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Donor**     | Make donations to charities and manage your giving portfolio                     |
| **Charity**   | Register your organization to receive donations and post volunteer opportunities |
| **Volunteer** | Browse service opportunities and build a verified contribution record            |

> **Note:** You can only select one primary role during registration. Contact support if you need multi-role access.

## Step 3 — Enter Your Details

Fill in the registration form:

- **Email address** — Used for login and notifications. Must be a valid, accessible email.
- **Password** — Minimum 8 characters. Use a mix of letters, numbers, and symbols.
- **Confirm password** — Re-enter your password to confirm.

For **Charity** accounts, you will also be asked for:

- Organization name
- EIN (Employer Identification Number)
- 501(c)(3) determination letter (upload required for verification)

## Step 4 — Verify Your Email

After submitting the form, check your inbox for a verification email from Give Protocol. Click the link in the email to confirm your address.

> If you don't receive the email within a few minutes, check your spam folder or click **Resend verification email** on the login page.

## Step 5 — Log In

Once your email is verified, go to [giveprotocol.io/login](https://giveprotocol.io/login), enter your credentials, and sign in.

## After Registration

- **Donors** — You'll be taken to [First Steps](./first-steps.md) to connect your wallet and make your first donation.
- **Charities** — Your account enters a verification queue. An administrator will review your 501(c)(3) documentation. You'll receive an email when your organization is approved (typically within 1–3 business days).
- **Volunteers** — You can immediately browse opportunities at `/opportunities`.

## Troubleshooting

| Problem                           | Solution                                        |
| --------------------------------- | ----------------------------------------------- |
| Didn't receive verification email | Check spam/junk; click Resend on the login page |
| Password reset                    | Click **Forgot password?** on the login page    |
| Wrong role selected               | Contact support to change your account type     |
| Charity verification delayed      | Email support with your EIN for a status update |
