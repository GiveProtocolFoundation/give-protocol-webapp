# Getting Started with Give Protocol

Give Protocol is a blockchain-powered charitable giving platform that lets you donate cryptocurrency or fiat currency to verified 501(c)(3) nonprofits, manage portfolio funds, and track your charitable impact—all from one place.

## Who Is Give Protocol For?

- **Donors** — Make crypto or fiat donations, manage recurring giving, and track your impact.
- **Charities** — Register your organization, receive donations, and manage volunteer opportunities.
- **Volunteers** — Browse opportunities, log hours, and build a verifiable service record.
- **Administrators** — Approve charities, manage users, and oversee platform operations.

## Prerequisites

Before you begin, make sure you have:

- A modern web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge)
- An email address for account registration
- Optionally: a crypto wallet (MetaMask, WalletConnect, Coinbase Wallet, etc.) if you plan to donate cryptocurrency

## Accessing Give Protocol

Visit [giveprotocol.io](https://giveprotocol.io) to open the application.

From the home page you can:

- Browse verified charities without creating an account
- Sign up as a donor, charity, or volunteer
- Connect your crypto wallet directly

## Quick Start by Role

### Donors

1. **[Create an account](./creating-account.md)** at `/register`
2. **[Set up your profile](./setting-up-profile.md)** with your giving preferences
3. **[Connect a wallet](./wallet-connection.md)** (required for crypto donations)
4. Browse charities at `/browse` and make your first donation
5. Monitor all giving in the **[Donor Dashboard](./dashboard.md)** at `/give-dashboard`

### Charities

1. **[Create an account](./creating-account.md)** and select _Charity_ as your role
2. Submit your 501(c)(3) details for verification
3. Once approved, manage your profile and causes in the Charity Portal at `/charity-portal`

### Volunteers

1. **[Create an account](./creating-account.md)** and select _Volunteer_ as your role
2. Browse open opportunities at `/opportunities`
3. Log hours and build your verified contribution history at `/contributions`
