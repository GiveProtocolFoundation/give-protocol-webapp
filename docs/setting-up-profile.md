# Setting Up Your Profile

After creating your account, take a few minutes to complete your profile. A complete profile helps charities understand your giving interests and lets you personalize your Give Protocol experience.

## Accessing Profile Settings

1. Log in to Give Protocol
2. Click your avatar or name in the top-right navigation
3. Select **Profile** or **Settings** from the dropdown

Alternatively, go directly to `/settings`.

## Donor Profile

### Personal Information

- **Display name** — This name appears on donation receipts and, optionally, on charity donor walls.
- **Profile photo** — Optional. Helps personalize your dashboard.
- **Country / Region** — Used for localized currency display and tax receipt formatting.

### Giving Preferences

- **Preferred currency** — Select the currency for displaying donation amounts (USD, EUR, GBP, etc.). This is display-only; actual token values are not converted automatically.
- **Preferred networks** — Choose which blockchain networks (Base, Optimism, Moonbeam) you plan to use most. This pre-selects your default chain when donating.
- **Notifications** — Configure email alerts for donation confirmations, recurring payment reminders, and impact reports.

### Privacy Settings

- **Anonymous giving** — Toggle this on if you prefer your donations not to appear on public charity donor walls.
- **Public profile** — Controls whether other users can view your donation history.

## Charity Profile

Charity profiles are public-facing and visible to potential donors.

### Organization Details

- **Organization name** — Full legal name as it appears on your 501(c)(3) determination letter.
- **Description** — A brief summary of your mission (up to 500 characters). Shown on charity listing cards.
- **Long description** — Full story of your organization. Supports rich text formatting.
- **Logo** — Upload a square image (minimum 200×200 px, PNG or JPG).
- **Cover image** — Optional banner shown on your charity detail page.
- **Website** — Your organization's primary website URL.
- **Contact email** — Public email address for donor inquiries.

### Causes

Add the specific causes your organization supports (e.g., education, housing, climate). Causes appear as searchable tags on the charity browser.

### Wallet Address

Enter the EVM wallet address where donations will be sent. This address must be a wallet you control. Double-check it carefully—donations sent to an incorrect address cannot be recovered.

## Volunteer Profile

- **Display name** — Shown on your public service record.
- **Skills** — Tag the skills you offer (e.g., teaching, construction, translation). Charities use these to match volunteers to opportunities.
- **Availability** — Indicate your general availability (weekdays, weekends, evenings).
- **Bio** — A short description of your volunteer background and interests.

## Saving Changes

Click **Save** at the bottom of each section. Changes take effect immediately. Profile photo and logo uploads may take a few seconds to process.
