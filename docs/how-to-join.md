# How to Join Give Protocol

Give Protocol is open to donors, charities, and volunteers. This page explains how each type of participant joins the platform.

## Joining as a Donor

Anyone can join Give Protocol as a donor in minutes.

### Requirements

- A valid email address
- A crypto wallet (for crypto donations) **or** a credit/debit card (for fiat donations)

### Steps

1. Go to [giveprotocol.io/register](https://giveprotocol.io/register)
2. Select **Donor** as your role
3. Enter your email address and create a password
4. Verify your email address by clicking the link in the confirmation email
5. Log in and optionally [connect your wallet](./wallet-connection.md)
6. Browse charities at `/browse` and make your first donation

There is no approval process for donors. Your account is active immediately after email verification.

---

## Joining as a Charity

Charities must be registered 501(c)(3) nonprofit organizations in the United States. International organizations may be accepted on a case-by-case basis — contact support for details.

### Requirements

- Valid 501(c)(3) determination letter from the IRS
- Employer Identification Number (EIN)
- An EVM-compatible wallet address to receive donations
- An authorized representative to complete registration

### Steps

1. Go to [giveprotocol.io/register](https://giveprotocol.io/register)
2. Select **Charity** as your role
3. Enter your organization's name, EIN, and contact email
4. Upload your 501(c)(3) determination letter
5. Verify your email address
6. Wait for administrator review (typically 1–3 business days)
7. Once approved, you'll receive a confirmation email and can access the Charity Portal

### What Happens During Review

Give Protocol administrators verify that:

- Your EIN matches IRS records
- Your determination letter is current and valid
- Your wallet address is a valid EVM address

You'll be notified by email when your application is approved or if additional information is needed.

## Joining as a Volunteer

Volunteers can register and start browsing opportunities immediately—no approval required.

### Requirements

- A valid email address

### Steps

1. Go to [giveprotocol.io/register](https://giveprotocol.io/register)
2. Select **Volunteer** as your role
3. Enter your email and create a password
4. Verify your email
5. Log in and browse opportunities at `/opportunities`
6. Apply for positions and start logging hours

## Sharing Give Protocol

Help grow the Give Protocol community:

- **Share a charity page** — Copy the URL from any charity's profile page (`giveprotocol.io/charity/{slug}`) and share it with your network
- **Embed a donation button** — Charities can request an embeddable widget for their own website by contacting support
- **Refer another charity** — If you know a 501(c)(3) that should be on Give Protocol, send them to the registration page

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Is Give Protocol free to use?**
Yes. There is no fee to register. A 0.5% platform fee is deducted automatically from each crypto donation to sustain operations.

**Can I use Give Protocol outside the United States?**
Donors from any country can participate. Charity registration is currently limited to US 501(c)(3) organizations.

**What if I want to be both a donor and a volunteer?**
Contact support after registration to enable multi-role access on your account.

**Is my personal information shared with charities?**
By default, your name may appear on a charity's donor list. You can enable **anonymous giving** in your profile settings to keep your identity private.
